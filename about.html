Agnese Pozzobon

(1999) is a freelance visual and graphic designer. Her work focuses on publishing, data design and the construction of identities.

She currently collaborates with Genera, a multidisciplinary studio engaged in digital research and image-making, and together with Pepe Spike she manages the communications for Maquette, a cultural programme by Sotterranea.

She also works with the TIPS research team at the University of Padova, visualising the impact of technoscientific issues within the public sphere, furthering her interest in the relationship between design and information.
